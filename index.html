What is Photogrammetry?

It's the technology that allows me to obtain precise measurements and create 3D models from photographs. Using drones and specialized cameras, I capture images from multiple angles and process them with advanced software. The result is georeferenced digital twins, orthophotos, and point clouds that provide invaluable spatial data.

My Workflow

  1. Consultation and Flight Plan: I understand your needs to design the most efficient data capture.
  2. Image Capture: I use drones and high-resolution cameras to ensure data quality and accuracy.
  3. Data Processing: I align the images, generate point clouds, and create 3D models, georeferencing everything for millimeter accuracy.
  4. Custom Deliverables: I provide the results in the format you need: interactive 3D models, orthophotos, contour maps, and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is photogrammetry?

With the right equipment and methodology, centimeter-level accuracy can be achieved, comparable to or even exceeding traditional methods in certain applications.

How long does it take to complete a project?

It depends on the scale and complexity, but data capture with drones is significantly faster than conventional surveying, drastically reducing field time.

What deliverables can I receive?

From interactive 3D models, point clouds, high-resolution orthomosaics, to classic CAD plans with contour lines and profiles.
